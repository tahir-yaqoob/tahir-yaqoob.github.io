01 - My Story

Not the path I was handed -
the one I chose.

My journey began with ECAT preparation alongside board exams. After scoring around 230/400 on my first attempt, overconfidence led to a drop to 217 on the second. My final aggregate of about 74% was not enough for UET Lahore's main campus, but it brought me to the Faisalabad campus and that turned out to be exactly where I needed to be.

I arrived in Faisalabad on 15 September 2025 with a bag, a dream, and Google Maps that failed me the moment the rickshaw driver asked for landmarks. The campus was deserted from flood closures, with overgrown grass, silent roads, and an abandoned feeling everywhere.

First semester was raw. I had zero computer background. Programming was alien. Yet every late night in the hostel, every wrong lab I walked into, and every quiz I prepared for with friends built something stronger. I ended with a 3.94 GPA and topped the class.
