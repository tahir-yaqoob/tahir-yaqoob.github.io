Setting Up the Database Lab

Muhammad Tahir Yaqoob | First practical database work

In the next database lab session, I approached our instructor, Dr. Bilal, and politely asked him to repeat the instructions for students who had missed the previous class. He was very cooperative and briefly explained the setup process again.

During that session, I was instructed to download and install MySQL Server and MySQL Workbench on my laptop. These tools were necessary for performing database tasks and practicing SQL queries.

I carefully followed the instructions and successfully installed the software. After the setup, I started performing basic operations such as creating databases, creating tables, and inserting data into them.

It was my first practical experience with a proper database management system, and it made database work feel exciting.

While working in the lab, I realized how important databases are in managing large amounts of structured data efficiently. This experience increased my interest in database systems and their real-world applications.
