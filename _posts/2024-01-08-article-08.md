The first quiz of Programming Fundamentals was approaching, and it was an important one because the course carried three credit hours. My friends and I started preparing seriously together.

Late nights were spent studying concepts, practicing problems, and reviewing material shared by Dr. Bilal, including his Hands-On Python notes. My friend Zain already had a small coding background, and studying with him helped me understand many concepts faster.

## The result

When the quiz results were announced, something surprising happened. Only three students in the entire class scored full marks, and I was one of them.

> Getting full marks in that first quiz gave me confidence I had never felt before.

That moment made me realize that even without a prior programming background, I could still succeed if I worked hard enough.
