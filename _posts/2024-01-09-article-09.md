As the semester progressed, the midterm examinations approached. The workload increased significantly, and students started taking their studies more seriously. Group study sessions became common in the hostel as my friends and I gathered to review lecture notes, discuss problems, and help each other understand difficult topics.

Dr. Bilal remained one of the most supportive instructors during this period. He was always available to answer questions, even outside class hours.

## The outcome

When the midterm results were announced, I had scored 35 out of 40 in Programming Fundamentals. It was a strong result and confirmed something important.

> Midterms showed me that I truly belonged in this program.

For the first time, I started believing that I could perform among the top students in the class.
